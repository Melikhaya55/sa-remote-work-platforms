## Prerequisites to Complete FIRST

1. **Payoneer Account**
   - Sign up at https://www.payoneer.com
   - Takes 2-3 business days to verify
   - Better exchange rates than PayPal (10-15% more ZAR)

2. **Updated Resume/CV**
   - Highlight: AI automation, n8n, LiteLLM, Docker, GCP, Supabase
   - Frame any data annotation experience (even personal projects)

3. **Clean Email Address**
   - Use professional email for applications
   - Avoid spam/inbox issues

4. **Consistent Internet Connection**
   - Most platforms require stable connection
   - Some have minimum speed requirements
