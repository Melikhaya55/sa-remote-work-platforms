## Payment Methods for South Africans

Most platforms support:
- **PayPal** - Most common, but watch fees
- **Payoneer** - Recommended for better exchange rates
- **Bank Transfer (Wire)** - Sometimes available but slower
- **Payoneer Mastercard** - Can be used as debit card

**Tip:** Open a Payoneer account as it often offers 10-15% better exchange rates than PayPal.

## Important Notes

- **Tax Implications:** South African freelancers must declare international income to SARS
- **Task Availability:** Most platforms have periods of high/low task availability
- **Consistency:** Building reputation takes time; don't expect full-time hours immediately
- **Multiple Accounts:** Consider joining 2-3 platforms for consistent work
